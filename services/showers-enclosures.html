Custom Shower Glass

Designed Around Your Bathroom

Every shower space is different. We custom-fabricate each glass enclosure to your exact measurements, with the style, hardware, and finish selected to match your bathroom.

Frameless Glass Enclosures

The clean, modern standard. 3/8" or 1/2" tempered glass with minimal hardware. Hinged doors, fixed panels, and neo-angle configurations available.

  • 3/8" or 1/2" tempered safety glass
  • Brushed nickel, oil-rubbed bronze, chrome, matte black hardware
  • Custom heights and widths to the 1/8"
  • Clear, frosted, and low-iron glass options

Semi-Frameless Glass Enclosures

A framed track at the top and bottom with frameless glass panels — the value-conscious choice that still looks sharp.

  • Available in sliding bypass and hinged configurations
  • Ideal for standard shower and tub-to-shower openings
  • Multiple glass patterns available

Framed Glass Enclosures

Full aluminum frame — the budget-friendly, durable option often specified for production home builds and rental properties.

  • Lower cost per unit — ideal for volume builds
  • Available in standard anodized finishes
  • Sliding bypass, pivot, and hinged door options

How It Works

From Measure to Install

1. Request Your Estimate

Call or fill out our form. Tell us the opening size, your preferred style, and your timeline. We'll respond within one business day.

2. Field Measurement

We schedule a field measure at your site. We verify plumb, level, and any quirks in your opening that affect the fabrication.

3. Fabrication & Install

Glass is cut and tempered to your specifications. Installation is typically 1–2 hours. We clean up, test the hardware, and walk you through care instructions.
